Lab4 • Canvas

1. Drawing paper

A small application where you can draw lines on a canvas. It also includes some features like a guidance line when drawing, and the ability to join automatically the previous point if you just click (without moving the mouse) somewhere in the canvas.
Note: This application can have unexpected results on mobile devices.

2. Canvas game

A small game where you have to hit the most targets as possible in only 10 seconds. Ready? ;)
